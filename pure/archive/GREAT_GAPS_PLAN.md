## The honesty of the arbiters (stated once)

These frontiers split into two kinds. Some have hard arbiters — a forced number checkable against
precise measurement (a particle, a coupling, a spectral line). Others are structural or
foundational — what the right account of measurement *is*, why nonlocal correlation does not carry
a signal — where the arbiter is consistency with established experiment and the dissolution of a
standing paradox rather than a new decimal. Each result states which kind it is. A structural
result claims to force the right *form*; it does not pretend to a measured digit it has not earned.
