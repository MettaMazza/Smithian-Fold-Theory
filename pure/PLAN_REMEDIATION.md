## The six work items, in dependency order

### 1. Attempt the generation count in the engine — the load-bearing item

**What the engine currently shows.** The corpus forces the interaction sector — the four forces and their characteristic constants. It does not currently force the matter sector: the fermion mass spectrum, the number of generations, or the CKM/PMNS mixing matrices. This is the mechanical reading, stated plainly.

**The construction to attempt (§20 — built and run, not pre-judged).** The framework has forced integer structure consensus calls un-derivable before: the colour count from the tripling fold's fibre (m = 3), the mediator count m²−1, the spatial dimension from orbital stability (d = 3). The reachable construction is whether an analogous fold-structural constraint forces a generation count. Candidate structural sources to build and run:
- the fold's fibre structure iterated (whether a three-fold layering of the binary/tripling fibre forces exactly three families);
- the relationship between the forced spatial dimension three and a generation count (whether the same orbital/flux constraint that forces d = 3 forces a family count);
- whether the chirality two-hand structure (D7c) composed with the colour triple forces a three-by-something family pattern.

Each is attempted in the permitted language and run. If a generation count is forced, it is registered as a new result and point 1 closes by derivation. If the engine does not force it, that is reported plainly as not-forced — not as a wall asserted from a prior, but as the engine's reading after the attempt — and the matter sector stands as the open frontier, which feeds item 2.

**This item is attempted first because its outcome decides item 2.**

### 2. Scope resolution across all documents — contingent on item 1

**The fork (§11.4 — the author decides).** If item 1 forces the matter sector, no scoping is needed. If item 1 does not, the mechanical fact is that the framework unifies the four interactions and does not currently force the matter sector, and the author chooses between (a) leaving the matter sector named as the open frontier and making every "unifies" claim precise as "unifies the four interactions," or (b) another direction the author sets.

**The work, on fork (a).** Make every unification claim across the paper, master, abstract, and book consistent: the four interactions are unified and their constants forced; the matter-sector spectrum is the named open frontier, stated openly (the framework's honesty about its own boundary is consistent with how it already names G, Ω, and the absolute scale as free). No claim is removed that the engine supports; the framing is made to match exactly what is forced.

### 3. Sharpen the W/Z–mixing tie into a registered discriminating prediction

**What the engine shows.** B6 forces the on-shell identity sin²θ_W + M_W²/M_Z² = 1 exactly at every depth, from the channel structure. The standard account treats the relation between the mixing and the mass ratio as scheme-dependent and does not force it.

**The construction.** State the forced tie as an explicit numerical prediction with the framework's own forced rung-spacing as the tolerance: the two separately-measured observables must satisfy the identity to the framework's forced resolution. Register it as a discriminating test (a forced relationship between two quantities the standard account measures independently), with the forced value fixed first and the measured values named as arbiters (§11.2). Builds directly on B6; reachable now.

### 4. Attempt relative depth-anchoring — built and run, not pre-judged

**What is settled.** B12 forces scale-invariance, shown by running: no absolute energy is forced, because identical physics holds at every absolute scale. This is a forced property, not a gap.

**The construction to attempt (§20).** Whether the framework forces a depth-to-depth *ratio* anchoring — tying the electroweak running depth to another framework-internal forced depth (the strong-sector confinement onset, the coupling-convergence depth of B8/B9, or the fold depth at which the spatial dimension three appears in B7) — is a framework question, attempted in the engine, never settled from a dimensional intuition. If a forced internal depth-ratio pins the electroweak depth relative to another forced depth, it is registered and the mixing contact sharpens from a free-scale crossing toward a relative-anchored one. If the engine does not force it, that is reported as the engine's reading after the attempt.

### 5. Strengthen D9p to an explicit limit statement
