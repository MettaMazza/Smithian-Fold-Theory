## The dependency order

The mass spectrum is built before the mixing matrices, because a mixing matrix relates mass eigenstates to interaction eigenstates: the mass eigenstates must exist before their relation to the interaction basis can be constructed. Within the mass spectrum, the single-fermion mass scale is built before the generation pattern, and the generation pattern before the inter-sector pattern. The neutrino sector is built after the charged-fermion sector, because its mass mechanism (whether the neutrino mass is the same displaced-vacuum mass-part or a distinct structure) is a further construction on the charged-fermion result.
